Here's a little bit about me.

For the last 10 years, I've been working in informal education at museums and libraries. I've always been interested in learning and sharing information with others, so in 2018 I joined my local LGBT Center's History Project steering committee, where I created their website for digital exhibits. This reignited my love for software engineering and how tech can help share amazing stories with a wider audeince. So now I'm here, looking for more opportunities where I can help connect people to amazing stories.
